A simple algorithm to swap two numbers:

Algorithm: Swap Two Numbers
Inputs:
1. num1 (number)
2. num2 (number)

Outputs:
1. Swapped values of num1 and num2

Steps:
1. READ num1 and num2 from user
2. STORE num1 in a temporary variable temp
3. ASSIGN num2 to num1
4. ASSIGN temp to num2
5. DISPLAY swapped values of num1 and num2

Example:

Input: num1 = 5, num2 = 10
Temp = 5
Num1 = 10
Num2 = 5
Output: num1 = 10, num2 = 5
